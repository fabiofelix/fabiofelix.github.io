Fabio Felix

ffd2011@nyu.edu

I am a Postdoc at Tandon School/NYU, working on projects in Augmented Reality, Explainable AI, and, more recently, Urban Science. I work with researchers from VIDA lab, under supervision of professor Claudio Silva.

During my Ph.D. (co-supervised by Moacir Ponti and Rosane Minghim) and Masters (supervised by Rosane Minghim) in Computer Science, I developed predictive models and visualization frameworks to support the identification and exploration of animal species recorded in the wild, helping soundscape analysis. I conducted these research in a program from ICMC/USP, working with researchers from VICG lab (currently GIVA). I hold a bachelor's in Computer Science from UESB.

Besides, I worked as a technical expert on a research project at ICMC/USP, also creating material and tools to explore animal sounds, and worked for 6+ years with development of healthcare systems (medical laboratory, specialized clinic, and hospital).
